DOES KUNSAN REALLY

NEED 'B.A.C.C.'?

Why?

It's no secret that there are growing racial tensions in the United States. Service members stationed overseas are not immune to this. It is our job as wingmen and fellow brothers/sisters in arms to create an environment where individuals seeking further understanding, information, and fellowship can feel valued. This group strives to open the dialogue between service members of all backgrounds to promote diversity and understanding to make this assignment just a little bit better.

Purpose

The purpose of the B.A.C.C. is to enhance the Kunsan AB community by educating and celebrating the achievements, accomplishments, and contributions of Black Americans, and to promote and maintain a spirit of espirit de corps among people of all races. B.A.C.C. will use it's position to further promote talks of racial diversity and inclusion within the Kunsan AB community.

Interested in Membership?

Anyone and everyone of every race and background is welcome.

Attending meetings is highly encouraged however, to become an offical member all that is required is to pay the yearly dues.

E-1 through E-4: $10 / E-5 & above: $20

Membership includes a free patch as well as access to the closed B.A.C.C. Facebook group to stay up to date with meetings, club information and discussions.
